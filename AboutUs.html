About Us

AMARA JEWELS

Jaipur (India) based jewellery supply company. We're the team of professionals with experience who understand customer needs and deliver requirements in appropriate time with suitable price and quality.

Our customers belong from chain of stores, resellers, wholesalers, online stores, and social media sellers from different parts of the world.
